Horizontal Gene Transfer

Is a known problem when trying to selectively genetically engineer bacteria which are part of a microbiome, causing all microbiome species to be exposed to the genetic information and potentially express it.

ORF tailoring

Taking into account the different innate preferences of cellular machinery in different mictobiome organisms, communique offers an automated method for re-coding of the ORF sequence to promote gene expression in wanted hosts within a microbiome while simultanously diminishing expression in unwanted hosts.

c

Automatic ORF Optimization

Based on CUB (codon usage bias) scores as a measure of cellular machinery preference, communique uses a novel computational approach for recoding of the ORF.
