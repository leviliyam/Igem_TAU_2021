Welcome To Communique!

Communique, is a technology which will allow us to take the genetic information passed on to bacterial communities and translate it to a language understood only by handpicked members of the community.
This will selectively target specific species to express the gene of interest, while avoiding transfer of the genes to unwanted species within the community.
By using this approach, we can ensure increased productivity in utilizing bacterial communities for various applications, while providing higher bio-safety standards and new possibilities for engineering microbiomes.
